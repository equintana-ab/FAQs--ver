How to Place Your Order

  1. Choose Your Item

    Browse our website and select your preferred product, or share the item link/details with a Personal Shopper.

  2. Provide Delivery Details

    Send the complete delivery address, the recipient’s full name, and contact number to ensure smooth processing.

  3. Select Your Payment Method

    Proceed with online payment using any of the available payment options:

    International Payments:

    • VISA
    • Mastercard
    • American Express
    • JCB

    Local Payments

    • BDO Unibank
    • BPI (Bank of the Philippine Islands)
    • HSBC
    • Metrobank
    • RCBC Bankard
    • VISA
    • Mastercard
    • American Express
    • JCB
    • GCash
    • GrabPay
    • BPI Online Transfer
  4. Receive Order Confirmation

    Once payment is successful, you will receive an order confirmation via email.

  5. Delivery & Installation (if applicable)

    Your order will be delivered to the recipient. If installation is included, our team will coordinate directly to schedule it.
