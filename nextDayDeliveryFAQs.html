Frequently Asked Questions

Here are quick answers to questions shoppers ask the most.

Next Day Delivery within Metro Manila is available through:

  • Selected online orders on abenson.com placed before 3:00 PM

Online orders placed after the cut-off time will be processed the next day, and delivery will follow the next available schedule. For more information, please contact us at shop@abenson.com.

Next Day Delivery is available for orders paid using the following methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD) (Maximum of P30,000 delivery fee included)
  • GCash
  • Straight/Installment Credit Card
  • Straight Debit Card

Yes. Next Day Delivery applies to online transactions of up to ₱100,000 for credit and debit card purchases, and Cash on Delivery is up to ₱30,000 only.

Orders exceeding this amount may follow a different delivery schedule.

Next Day Delivery is currently available for Metro Manila addresses only.

Not all products are eligible. Availability depends on:

  • Product size and handling requirements
  • Item availability
  • Delivery location

For online orders, you will receive order updates via email or SMS once your order has been processed and scheduled for delivery. You may check the delivery status of your order through our Order Tracker.
