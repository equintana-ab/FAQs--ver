Using a power bank won’t let you run out of battery while outside or traveling. It is portable and convenient for charging multiple devices.

  • Capacity: 10,000–20,000mAh (higher mAh = more charges)
  • Fast charging support
  • Multiple USB ports for charging several devices at once
  • Ultra portable
  • Built-in cable → best for convenience
  • Magnetic / MagSafe → best for iPhone users
  • Laptop power banks → best for work trips

mAh (milliampere-hour) measures the capacity of the battery, or how much charge it can provide.

  • 5,000mAH: compact, great for emergency top-ups
  • 10,000mAh: Suitable for daily use. Charges most phones 1.5-2 times.
  • 20,000mAh: Best for travel, heavy users and multiple devices
